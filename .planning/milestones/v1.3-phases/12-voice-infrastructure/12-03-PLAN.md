  <name>Task 2: Create voice clone in ElevenLabs and add real credentials to .env</name>
  <what-to-do>
**Step 1: Record voice samples**

Record 3-5 short voice clips using iPhone Voice Memos:
- Each clip: 30 seconds to 3 minutes of natural speech
- Content: Read aloud naturally in Hebrew (e.g., a paragraph from a news article, or just speak naturally about any topic)
- Environment: Quiet room, phone held naturally — same quality you'd use for a voice memo to a friend
- Export as M4A or MP3 (Voice Memos default is fine)

**Step 2: Create the voice clone in ElevenLabs**

1. Go to [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io) and log in
2. Navigate to: **Voice Lab** -> **Add Generative or Cloned Voice** -> **Instant Voice Cloning**
3. Upload your 3-5 recordings
4. Name the voice `Yuval` (or any name you'll recognize)
5. Click **Create voice**
6. Wait a few seconds — the clone is instant

**Step 3: Test Hebrew pronunciation (quality gate)**

In the Voice Lab with your cloned voice selected:
1. Select model: `eleven_multilingual_v2`
2. Generate speech for these 5 test sentences:
   - "שלום, מה שלומך היום?"
   - "אני רוצה לשלוח לך הודעה קצרה."
   - "תודה רבה על העזרה שלך."
   - "האם אתה פנוי לדבר עכשיו?"
   - "להתראות, נדבר בקרוב."
3. Listen to each — quality bar: recognizable as your voice, Hebrew pronunciation natural

**Step 4: Copy the Voice ID**

1. In Voice Lab, click on your cloned voice
2. Click **ID** or find the Voice ID (looks like: `abc123def456...`)
3. Copy it

**Step 5: Get your API key**

1. Go to: **My Account** (top right) -> **API Keys**
2. Create a new key or copy an existing one

**Step 6: Update .env**

Edit `/home/yuval/whatsapp-bot/.env` and replace the placeholder values:
